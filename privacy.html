数据收集

Data Collection

我们不收集任何用户数据。

We do not collect any user data.

您复制到 ClipShelf 的所有内容（文本、图片、文件）均存储在您的本地设备上。我们：

All content you copy to ClipShelf (text, images, files) is stored locally on your device. We:

  • ❌ 不将数据上传到任何服务器
  • ❌ Do not upload data to any server
  • ❌ 不与第三方共享您的数据
  • ❌ Do not share your data with third parties
  • ❌ 不使用任何分析工具追踪您的使用

  • ❌ 不显示任何广告
  • ❌ Do not display any advertisements

数据存储

Data Storage

您的数据存储在以下位置：

Your data is stored in the following locations:

  • 存储路径：~/Library/Application Support/com.crossoverjie.ClipShelf/
  • Storage Path: ~/Library/Application Support/com.crossoverjie.ClipShelf/
  • 存储技术：SwiftData 本地数据库
  • Storage Technology: SwiftData local database
  • 图片存储：本地文件系统（外部存储）
  • Image Storage: Local file system (external storage)

您可以随时在应用内删除所有数据，或通过删除应用彻底清除数据。

You can delete all data within the app at any time, or completely remove it by deleting the app.

系统权限

System Permissions

ClipShelf 请求以下 macOS 系统权限：

ClipShelf requests the following macOS system permissions:

  • 辅助功能权限（Accessibility）：仅用于"快速粘贴"功能，模拟 ⌘V 按键将内容粘贴到目标应用
  • Accessibility Permission: Only used for the "Quick Paste" feature to simulate ⌘V keystrokes
  • 文件访问权限：仅用于拖放文件功能，访问您手动选择的文件
  • File Access Permission: Only used for drag-and-drop file functionality
  • 启动登录权限：仅用于"开机自动启动"功能
  • Login Items Permission: Only used for "Launch at Login" feature

Subscriptions & Payments

In-app purchases (ClipShelf Pro) are processed through Apple StoreKit:

  • We cannot access your payment details

  • Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID
